You're a Bum!

Congratulations! You are a bum. You are embracing all college has to offer. Well, except for the academics. You probably spend your weeknights out at Smokes, forgetting you even have class the next day. Not that the professors would mind if you didn't show up. Or maybe you're the type too busy playing Super Smash Bros to walk all the way to the library to study. The best case scenario is you're a senior who couldn't care less about classes anymore. Cheers to hoping we can all be that person some day.
