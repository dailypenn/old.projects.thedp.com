The Daily Pennsylvanian is a $3-million, student-run media organization. Our incredible teams work together to market our products and sustain our business. Through the DP, students experience working for a real company, with real staffers, clients, and revenues. Join one of our teams to gain an invaluable, hands-on experience unlike any other.

The Daily Pennsylvanian's Advertising Department offers students hands-on consulting experience with local and national clients.

Advertising representatives work directly with business owners to help recognize, pinpoint and provide solutions to the problems that they are facing within their business. With the help of our outstanding analytics and marketing teams, advertising representatives consult with their clients to determine the best ways to reach the Penn community.

Advertising representatives are paid for their work with commission rates that can go up to 20%, as well as bonus incentives. Time commitment is generally about 8-10 hours a week.

34th Street Magazine

34th Street Magazine is your authority on Penn student life. We are a student magazine that empowers student voices, sheds light on student issues, and provides timely commentary on campus culture and arts. The opportunities at Street are endless. You can write cover stories, help envision marketing campaigns, design and lay out issues, attend press movie screenings, and so much more.

34th Street Magazine is a hub for Penn voices and narrative, reported arts, culture, and feature content. We are looking for writers, photographers, videographers, and social media whizzes to create, package, and promote Street to the Penn audience. Writers can write for our Features, Style, Arts, Film & TV, Music, or Ego sections. Multimedia staffers work with either photo or video departments. And our Audience Engagement Associates manage our social media and our newsletter, The Toast.

Street gives writers the opportunity to be creative while pursuing journalism, lets Audience Engagement Associates learn how to build and promote a brand online, and teaches photographers and videographers to create engaging editorial visuals. Join Street to be part of a tight–knit community and learn from some of the most interesting and dedicated people at Penn—we promise you won’t regret it.
