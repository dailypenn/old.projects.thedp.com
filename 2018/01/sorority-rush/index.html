January 9

Open House

Location: Annenberg Auditorium

Time: 10am-10pm

Description: The PNMs visit each of the eight sorority houses for 30 minutes, with several breaks during the day. Starting this round, PNMs submit their preferences on which sororities they want to continue to see, and sororities submit their preferences too at the end of the day.

January 10

Sisterhood Round One

Location: Group Location

Time: 5:30pm-10:15pm

Description: The PNMs receive a narrowed list of houses to visit. They spend 45 minutes at each house, taking about sisterhood, financial obligations, and GPA requirements.

January 11

Sisterhood Round Two

Location: Group Location

Time: 5:30pm-10:15pm

Description: A continuation of Sisterhood Round I.

January 12

Philanthropy Day

Location: Group Location

Time: 3pm-10pm

Description: The PNMs receive a narrowed list of houses to visit. They spend an hour at each house, talking about each sorority’s philanthropic efforts.

January 13

Preference Round

Location: Group Location

Time: 3:30pm-7:15pm

Description: The PNMs go through the last round of houses before bid day. Each house event is an hour long and is a “beautiful ceremonial experience.”

January 14

Bid Day

Location: Annenberg Auditorium

Time: 2:30pm

Description: The PNMs receive their official invitations, or “bids,” to join one of the sororities.
